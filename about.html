About the Company

Macaron Desserts is a cookie catering company specializing in French-style macarons.

Check out the menu page for a combination of seasonal and year-round flavors.


Bianca Grant

Hi, i'm the owner of and chef at Macaron Desserts. I grew up and am based in San Luis Obispo, CA. I was educated in business at Cuesta College and took classes in economics at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. My passion for baking started as a child. One of my earliest baking memories is making cinnamon rolls with my next-door neighbor. Since then, I have been working on my recipes and found making macarons a challenging yet satisfying process.
